Critical Discussion of Technology and Applications

Social Implications:

Ethical Lens 1 - Privacy Concerns:

AR/VR technologies often collect and use user data for personalization and tracking. Concerns arise regarding data privacy, consent, and potential misuse of personal information.

Ethical Lens 2 - Inclusivity and Accessibility:

VR headsets, in particular, may exclude individuals with certain disabilities. Ensuring inclusive design and accessibility features remains a challenge in AR/VR development.

Mitigating Harms and Recommendations:

Technological Strategies:

  • Implement robust privacy measures, emphasizing transparent data usage and giving users control over their data collection.
  • Develop AI-driven solutions to enhance inclusivity, making AR/VR interfaces adaptable to diverse needs.

Social Strategies:

  • Advocate for policies ensuring data protection and user privacy in AR/VR applications.
  • Encourage collaboration between tech developers and accessibility experts to create more inclusive experiences.
